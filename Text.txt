Diabetes (Type 2)

Metrics: Glucose level, BMI, age, blood pressure, insulin, family history.

Heart Disease / Cardiovascular Disease

Metrics: Cholesterol, blood pressure, ECG results, chest pain type, age, resting heart rate.

Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Metrics: Age, weight, BMI, lifestyle habits, family history.

Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD)

Metrics: Serum creatinine, blood urea, hemoglobin, blood pressure, potassium levels.

Liver Disease (e.g., Hepatitis, Cirrhosis, Fatty Liver)

Metrics: ALT/AST levels, bilirubin, albumin, alkaline phosphatase, age.

Stroke Risk

Metrics: Blood pressure, cholesterol, age, diabetes history, smoking habits.

Parkinson’s Disease

Metrics: Voice measurements, hand tremor readings, motor function tests.

Alzheimer’s Disease (early detection)

Metrics: Cognitive test scores, memory scores, demographic features, genetic factors (like APOE).

Cancer Risk Prediction (e.g., Breast Cancer)

Metrics: Tumor size, cell shape/texture (from biopsy), age, family history.

COVID-19 Severity / Outcome Prediction

Metrics: Oxygen saturation, CRP, D-dimer, age, comorbidities, chest CT scores.
